# Style: Portfolio

Writing voice of Miles Zeilstra. Professional student presenting their work. Not LinkedIn, not WhatsApp.

## Sentence Structure

- Short-medium-short rhythm. Never three long sentences in a row.
- Lead with the point, no build-up: "Alles zelf gebouwd:" not "Ik heb ervoor gekozen om alles zelf te bouwen"
- Colon for elaboration: "Alles zelf opgezet: database, authenticatie, het hele MVC patroon"
- Comma-compound for speed: "Je selecteert je pool, ziet direct je zwakke plekken en krijgt suggesties"
- No em dashes. Use commas, periods, or colons instead.

## Word Choice

- Active verbs: "bouwde", "opgezet", "gescrapet". Never "was verantwoordelijk voor" or "gericht op".
- Tech terms stay in English, rest follows input language: "matchup engine", "MVC patroon", "scrapen"
- No adjective stacking. Never "gepolijste, data-gedreven, responsieve tool". One descriptor max.
- Avoid these words/phrases entirely:
  - "volledig", "compleet", "professioneel", "gepolijst"
  - "gericht op", "verantwoordelijk voor", "in staat om"
  - "data-gedreven", "gestroomlijnd", "robuust", "schaalbaar"
  - "van concept tot deployment", "van A tot Z"
  - "passie voor", "energie van krijg", "gedreven door"
  - "polished", "robust", "scalable", "streamlined", "cutting-edge"
  - "responsible for", "focused on", "aimed at"
  - "from concept to deployment", "end-to-end"

## Structure

- Show, don't claim. Instead of "ik ben goed in features bedenken", tell the DraftGap example.
- Specific over vague: "winrate data van U.GG" not "externe databronnen"
- No filler transitions. "Daarnaast" and "Bovendien" at most once per text.
- No overdoing, no underselling. State facts.

## Tone

- Student presenting work at a demo. Professional but natural.
- Honest about scope: "Mijn eerste grote Laravel project" not "een ambitieus full-stack platform"
- No sentimentality: not "hier heb ik echt leren kennen" or "dat is waar ik energie van krijg"
- No marketing speak: not "een tool die spelers helpt betere beslissingen te maken"

## Examples

**Bad**: "Ik was verantwoordelijk voor de volledige technische realisatie van het gepolijste, data-gedreven platform, van concept tot deployment."

**Good**: "Alles zelf gebouwd: van het scrapen van de data tot de matchup engine en de React frontend."

**Bad**: "A polished, data-driven tool that helps players make better draft decisions. Features real-time matchup analysis, responsive mobile design, and shareable pool links."

**Good**: "You add your pool, instantly see your weak spots and get suggestions to fix them. Works on mobile and you can share your pool via a link."
